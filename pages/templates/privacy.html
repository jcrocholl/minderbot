Privacy policy

Minderbot protects your privacy. If you use this service, the following information may be stored on our servers:

  1. Your email address.
  2. Your password, encrypted using SHA-1 with salt.
  3. Your IP address, to prevent multiple votes for feedback.
  4. Your session cookie.
  5. Your reminders.

Your information listed above is private and will not be shown to other users of the Minderbot service. However, it will be available to Minderbot and Google engineers because it is stored in the App Engine Datastore.

This policy incorporates the Google App Engine privacy notice:
